Our Story

Greenwood Community Library has been serving the community since 1975. Our mission is to foster a love for reading, provide access to knowledge, and create a welcoming space for all.

We offer a wide range of resources, including books, e-books, audiobooks, and community programs. Whether you're a student, a parent, or a lifelong learner, we have something for you.
